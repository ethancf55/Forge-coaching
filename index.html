Fit check

Who this is for

Built for young men who already lift and want a coach who treats training like a craft — not a content funnel.

You’re a good fit if…

  • You’re roughly 18–28 and take the gym seriously
  • You want structure: programming, progression, and feedback — not random workouts
  • You’re natural, using peptides, or enhanced — and want coaching that doesn’t pretend otherwise
  • You’ll train consistently and send check-ins (effort > motivation quotes)
  • You care about long-term strength, physique, and recovery — not 30-day gimmicks

This isn’t for you if…

  • You want a magic plan with zero effort
  • You’re looking for medical advice, sourcing, or protocol design for PEDs
  • You need a general “get healthy” program with no lifting background
  • You only want passive PDFs and no accountability
  • You’re not ready to train most weeks of a 12-week block

Honesty note: I program training and lifestyle support around your reality. I don’t sell “natty transformations” that weren’t, and I don’t coach PED use. If you enhance, we train and recover like adults — no lectures, no pretending.

FAQ

Straight answers

Do you only coach natural lifters?

No. I work with natural lifters, lifters using peptides, and enhanced lifters. I don’t design or recommend drug protocols — that’s outside my scope. I do program training and recovery honestly around your situation.

What’s included in the $349 / 12 weeks?

Custom programming for the full block, check-ins, messaging, form feedback, and adjustments based on progress and recovery. Details are on the Offer section above.

What happens after 12 weeks?

You can stop with no pressure, or continue at $179/month with Ongoing Coaching. Same coach, updated plan, month-to-month with 30 days notice to cancel.

Do I need a commercial gym?

A solid gym setup helps, but we program around what you have — commercial gym, home gym, or a limited rack. Tell me your equipment when you apply.

Is there a free call?

Yes. After you apply, we do a short intro call to confirm fit before you pay. No credit card required to apply.
